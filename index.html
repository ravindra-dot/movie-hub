“Inception” (2010) – is a visually stunning sci-fi thriller by Christopher Nolan that blurs the line between dreams and reality. With breathtaking visuals, complex layers of storytelling, and a haunting score, “Inception” challenges your perception of what’s real and what’s imagined.
